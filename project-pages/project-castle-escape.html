Overview

Castle Escape is a strategy, adventure game designed to create a fun but challenging experience. Players must plan paths to move through the zombies without getting hit and time their movements to perfection to collect keys and escape the castle. I was inspired by "The World's Hardest Game" and wanted to make a similar game enhanced with more of a story and my own assets.
